# CampusClip — Mid-August Launch Campaign
**Campaign window:** August 15 – September 7, 2026
**Launch date:** August 19, 2026 (app goes live)
**Target:** Western University students, 18–22

---

## Campaign Overview

The pre-launch warm-up (August 1–14) built awareness. This campaign does three things:

1. **Final countdown (Aug 15–18)** — create urgency and get Western students to follow before launch
2. **Launch moment (Aug 19–21)** — make the launch feel like an event, drive downloads fast
3. **First week of semester push (Sep 1–7)** — hit students when they actually need the app

The cold start is the biggest risk. The strategy: make the first week of downloads so dense that every student who opens the app finds classmates already there.

---

## The One Rule for This Campaign

Every post ends with one of three CTAs, never more than one:
1. **"link in bio"** — for download-focused posts
2. **"follow so you know when it drops"** — for pre-launch posts
3. **"tag a Western student"** — for share-focused posts

No post has two CTAs. No post says "like, follow, share, and download." Pick one thing and ask for it.
