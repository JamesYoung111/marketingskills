WHAT CAMPUSCLIP ACTUALLY DOES

Syllabus Scanner
Point your camera at your course syllabus on day one. CampusClip reads every assignment, every due date, and every grade weight and builds your semester automatically. Not just a calendar — a full grade tracker that knows how much each thing is worth.

Grade Tracker
Open CampusClip and see your exact weighted grade in every course — right now, not when your professor gets around to updating Canvas. See where you stand, what's left in the semester, and what you need to hit your target.

Class Social Layer
Every class you join has a shared space. See who else is in your class, start conversations, coordinate on group projects, and ask questions without hunting people down on Instagram or starting a new group chat from scratch every semester.

Campus Events & Clubs
Find out what's actually happening at your university — club events, campus events, things your friends are going to — without relying on word of mouth or remembering to check a poster board.

WHO IT'S FOR
