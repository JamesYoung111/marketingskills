# CampusClip Cold Outreach — Western University Launch
*August 2026 Pre-Launch Outreach*

---

## Overview

Three cold email sequences targeting the people with the most reach on campus: club presidents, student union reps, and student content creators. The goal is not a formal partnership pitch — it is getting the right people genuinely interested in the app, using it, and naturally telling the people around them about it.

Every sequence is written peer-to-peer. No startup language. No pitch decks attached. No asking for a 30-minute call. The ask is small and the value is real.

**Timing:** Send first emails the week of July 28, 2026 — four weeks before the August launch window. This gives time to build relationships before the campus is full.

---

## Persona A: Club Presidents

**Who they are:** Students running clubs of 20–200 members. They coordinate events, post in group chats, manage email lists, and are genuinely exhausted by the logistics of keeping everyone informed. They understand what it means to have information scattered across six different places.

**Why they matter:** A club president who uses CampusClip and tells their exec team is potentially 5–15 people on the app before a single ad runs. If they post about it in their club group chat, that's another 50–100.

**The angle:** CampusClip solves a real problem for them specifically — coordinating with members, sharing events, keeping people informed. They are not just users, they are infrastructure.

## Outreach Logistics

### Finding contacts

- **Club presidents:** Western's campus club directory lists club names and contact emails. Cross-reference with the USC (University Students' Council) clubs list.
- **Student union reps:** USC executive team and faculty society presidents are listed publicly on Western's USC website.
- **Creators:** Search Instagram and TikTok for "Western University," "uwo," "life at Western," "western ontario student" — look for accounts with recent posts and consistent Western-specific content.

### Volume targets

- Club presidents: 40–60 outreach (aim for 15–20 clubs set up before launch)
- Student union reps: 10–15 outreach (USC exec, orientation leaders, faculty society presidents)
- Creators: 20–30 outreach (aim for 8–12 who post before or at launch)

### Sending approach

Send these from a personal email or a simple [yourname@campusclip.app] address — not a mass email tool. These should look like individual messages, because they are. Do not CC anyone. Do not BCC a list. Write each subject line as-is, lowercase, no punctuation.

### Tracking

Keep a simple spreadsheet: Name, Persona, Email, Date Sent, Reply Y/N, Outcome. Follow up only once after no reply (the sequence above handles this). If someone says no, mark it and move on — no re-engagement.

### What success looks like

- 15–20 clubs set up before launch
- 3–5 student union reps who have early access and are willing to mention the app at orientation
- 8–12 creators who post about the app in the first two weeks of September
