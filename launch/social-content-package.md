| AI avatar option | **HeyGen** | If you want to produce at scale without filming every day |
| B-roll | **Veo / Runway** | For campus/student lifestyle shots you can't film |

### Filming Rules for Student Audience

1. **Lo-fi outperforms polished on TikTok** — a shaky genuine reaction from a real Western student beats a produced ad every time
2. **Always add captions** — 85% of TikTok is watched on mute (CapCut auto-captions in 30 seconds)
3. **Film the app working, not the app existing** — show the syllabus scan happening, not a screenshot of the result
4. **Face cam in corner of screen recordings** — adds 15–25% to completion rate
5. **9:16 aspect ratio** for all TikTok and Reels — never post 1:1 on TikTok
6. **Original audio for tutorials and stories** — trending sounds for relatable/meme content

In order of conversion impact:
