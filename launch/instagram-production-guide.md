## Canva Template Library

Save these 8 templates in Canva. Every post below uses one of them.

---

### Template A — BOLD STATEMENT (Light)
**Use for:** relatable problem posts, awareness content
- Background: `#F7F4EF` (warm cream)
- Top-left: thin vertical purple bar (8px wide, 240px tall)
- Headline: Poppins Bold, 96–120pt, `#1C1C2E`, left-aligned, 2–3 lines
- Second line (optional): same size, `#6C63FF`
- Divider: 2px horizontal line, `#D0CAFF`, full width
- Body: Poppins Regular, 40pt, `#64748B`, below divider
- Tag pill (top): rounded rectangle, fill `#6C63FF`, white Poppins Bold 26pt text
- CTA button (bottom-left): rounded rectangle, fill `#6C63FF`, white text
- Decoration: large soft circle (opacity 15%) top-right corner, `#6C63FF`
- Logo: bottom-left, purple CC badge + "CampusClip" wordmark

---

### Template B — HERO DARK
**Use for:** countdown posts, urgency, launch run-up
- Background: deep navy gradient `#0D1117` → `#060C14`
- Accent bar: 100px wide purple rectangle, top-left
- Headline: Poppins Bold, 110pt, white, left-aligned
- Body: Poppins Regular, 42pt, `#94A3B8`
- Glow effect: two large soft circles (opacity 20–30%) in purple, one top-right, one bottom-left
- CTA button: rounded rectangle, fill `#6C63FF`, white text
- Logo: bottom-left, white wordmark

**Canva tip:** Use a dark gradient background element + add two large circles, set blend mode to Screen, reduce opacity.

---

### Template C — LAUNCH / FULL PURPLE
**Use for:** launch day, announcements, high-energy moments
- Background: gradient `#5540F0` → `#9060F8` (top to bottom)
- Large overlapping circles: white at 8% opacity top-right, dark at 15% bottom-left
- Headline: Poppins Bold, 150pt, white — massive and dominant
- Divider line: white at 30% opacity
- Body: Poppins Regular, 46pt, `#DDD4FF`
- CTA button: white pill, purple text — the only button on the post
- Logo: bottom-left, white

**Canva tip:** Search "purple gradient" in Canva backgrounds. Add a large white circle element top-right, set opacity to 8%.

---

### Template D — SPLIT PANEL
**Use for:** before/after, problem vs solution, comparison
- Top 38%: solid `#4B44CC` (deep purple)
- Bottom 62%: `#F7F4EF` (cream)
- Top text: Poppins Bold, 86pt, white, left-aligned — the problem/statement
- Bottom headline: Poppins Bold, 72pt, `#1C1C2E`
- Bottom body: Poppins Regular, 38pt, `#64748B`
- Decorative circle: straddling the split line, right side, `#6C63FF` at 50% opacity
- Logo: bottom-left on cream section

---

### Template E — QUOTE CARD
**Use for:** testimonials, emotional posts, social proof
- Background: near-white `#FAFAF7`
- Giant decorative quotation mark: 280pt, `#EAE5FF` (very light purple), top-left
- Attribution pill: top-right, purple background, white text
- Quote: Poppins Bold, 74pt, `#1C1C2E`, left-aligned, vertically centered
- Accent bar: 80px wide, 6px tall, `#6C63FF`, below quote
- Subtext: Poppins Regular, 36pt, `#64748B`
- Logo: bottom-left

**Canva tip:** Use the Text tool to add a huge " character. Set it to Poppins Bold, colour `#EAE5FF`, about 280pt. Layer your quote text on top.

---

### Template F — BIG NUMBER
**Use for:** countdown numbers, milestones, stats
- Background: dark (Template B style) OR cream (Template A style)
- Large concentric circles: 3 rings behind the number, purple outline, decreasing opacity
- Number: Poppins Bold, 300pt, centred — takes up most of the canvas
- Label: Poppins Bold, 70pt, centred below number, `#A99BFF`
- Subtext: Poppins Regular, 38pt, centred, `#94A3B8`, 3 lines max
- Logo: bottom-left

**Canva tip:** Add three circle outlines (no fill, purple stroke), different sizes, stack them centred. Your number goes in the middle.

---

### Template G — TWO COLUMN
**Use for:** direct comparisons, meme formats
- Left half: `#10131A` (near black)
- Right half: `#F7F4EF` (cream)
- Centre divider: 4px, `#6C63FF`
- Left label: Poppins Bold, 28pt, `#94A3B8`, centred at top
- Right label: Poppins Bold, 28pt, `#6C63FF`, centred at top
- Left items: Poppins Regular, 36pt, `#94A3B8`, centred
- Right items: Poppins Bold, 36pt, `#1C1C2E`, centred
- Logo: bottom-left

---

### Template H — CHECKLIST
**Use for:** how-it-works, step-by-step, practical content
- Background: `#F7F4EF`
- Left edge: full-height 14px bar, `#6C63FF`
- Title: Poppins Bold, 68pt, `#1C1C2E`
- Divider line below title: `#DDD8F0`
- Each item: numbered circle (filled purple, white number), Poppins Regular 40pt text right of circle

## Story Templates

Stories are 1080×1920. Keep text in the middle third — top and bottom get cut off by the UI.

### Story Type 1 — COUNTDOWN
- Dark background (Template B colours)
- Add Canva's built-in Countdown Sticker
- 1–2 lines of text: "X days until CampusClip drops at Western"
- Purple CTA button linking to bio

### Story Type 2 — POLL
- Cream or purple background
- Question in large bold text
- Add Instagram Poll sticker

### Story Type 3 — LINK SWIPE
- Single clear CTA
- Arrow pointing down to link sticker
- High contrast — people decide in 0.5 seconds

### Story Type 4 — STAT UPDATE
- Dark background
- Big number centred
- "X students in X hours" style copy
- Feels like a live update

### Story Type 5 — QUOTE SHARE
- Reposts a feed quote post
- Add a "swipe up" or link sticker to App Store
