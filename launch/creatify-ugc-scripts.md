## Creatify Setup Notes

- **Avatar**: Choose a 19-22 year old who looks like a Canadian university student
- **Background**: Dorm room, library, or campus coffee shop setting
- **Captions**: ON — large, bold, high contrast (many watch without sound)
- **Format**: 9:16 vertical (TikTok/Reels)
- **Duration**: Keep under 30s for best completion rate
- **B-roll**: Insert app screenshots (from `launch/app-screenshots/`) at the timestamps where the solution is described
